London with Kids

Mini mornings and Odean kids is great for weekends but what about the school holidays? Each cinema offer a variety of discounts for customers of affiliated businesses.

Tow Vue Tickets for £7

Vodafone mobile customers can get two adult tickets for £7 (£3.50 each) at most Vue cinemas to see any 2D movie each week through the My Vodafone app.

How to get the deal

  • Download or sign into the free My Vodafone App, select the 'VeryMe' button to access a list of available offers and choose the Vue deal.
  • Visit the Vue link within the deal and you'll be able to claim a promo code for two tickets for £7.
  • You'll be emailed two e-codes which you can exchange at the box office or online (but not over the phone)
