London with Kids

Going to the cinema once you're a parent becomes a thing of the past, here are our tips on how you can start enjoying the cinema again but with your children this time.

Vue Cinema Mini Mornings

Vue Cinema Mini Morning sessions give both adults and kids the chance to experience big screen entertainment for a very small price. Mini Mornings are run every Saturday and Sunday morning from 10am, plus every day in the school holidays (with availability likely to change depending on when your child’s holidays occur). Showing kids movies and new kids films, Vue's Mini Mornings ensures fun in the cinema for all the family.

Opening times, location and pricing

  • Opening Dates & Times Saturdays and Sundays term time. See local cinema for school holiday dates.
  • Location 10 locations including Westfield Stratford, Piccadilly and Islington
  • Price Both adults and children pay from £2.49/€2.99 per ticket.
  • Website https://www.myvue.com/offers/mini-mornings

Odean Kids

Entertain the family for less! Those rainy day weekends can sometimes be tough to keep the kids entertined. With Odean Kids screenings everyone in the family can enjoy the best children movies for kids ticket prices! Check your local cinema for ODEON Kids screenings, which happen every Saturday and Sunday and every day during the school holidays.

Opening times, location and pricing

  • Opening Dates & Times Saturdays and Sundays term time. See local cinema for school holiday dates.
  • Location 18 locations including Greenwich, Covent Garden and Leicester Square
  • Price Prices range from £6.25 upwards - remember even adults pay kids price!
  • Website https://www.odeon.co.uk/offers-membership/odeon-kids/
